Air Traffic Control Scanner.

Discover the fascinating world of aviation communication with AirRadio, the premier app for listening to live air traffic control (ATC) communications. Whether you are at the airport and curious about the reasons behind flight delays, live near an airport and want to stay informed about ongoing operations, or have always been intrigued by the conversations between pilots and air traffic controllers, AirRadio is your go-to solution.

Key Features:

  • Global Coverage: Access a comprehensive list of airports from over 100 countries, ensuring you can tune in to ATC communications from virtually anywhere in the world.

  • Favorites: Save your most frequently listened-to airports for quick and easy access.

  • Background Listening: Continue to listen to ATC communications even when using other apps or when your device's screen is off, providing uninterrupted access to live updates.

  • Weather Updates: Stay informed with the most accurate and up-to-date weather information directly from the ATC communications, essential for both aviation enthusiasts and professionals.

  • Regular Updates: Our airport list is continuously updated to include new locations and ensure the best listening experience.

How It Works:

Simply select your desired channel, press play, and wait a few seconds to start enjoying real-time ATC Radio.

Why Choose AirRadio?

AirRadio allows you to immerse yourself in the world of aviation, gaining insights into the operations of aircraft and ground services at various airports. Whether you are an aviation enthusiast, a frequent traveler, or someone who simply enjoys learning about the intricacies of air traffic control, AirRadio offers an unparalleled experience.
